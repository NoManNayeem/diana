About Diana

Diana is an LLM-agnostic, voice-enabled assistant designed for natural, contextual conversations. It combines modern UI/UX, multi-agent orchestration, and extensible tooling to help you get more done.

LLM Agnostic

Bring your own model/provider. The architecture keeps the assistant flexible and future-proof.

Tooling & MCP

Model Context Protocol and tool integrations enable powerful workflows beyond plain chat.

Privacy by Design

Clear separation of frontend and backend; environment-driven config and secure defaults.
